# World Knowledge Championship 2026: Official Call for Entries

This repository houses the complete compendium of 330 specialized calls for entries for the **World Knowledge Championship 2026**. 

## Objective
The World Knowledge Championship is a premier global initiative designed to identify, assess, and integrate top-tier talent and innovative proposals across a wide spectrum of academic, engineering, and management disciplines. Through these targeted calls, we invite leading experts, researchers, and professionals to contribute their specialized knowledge toward shaping high-impact solutions to global challenges.

## Participation and Scope
The 158 calls for applications encompass a rigorous and transparent evaluation process across various fields. Each document details the specific criteria, focus areas, and strategic goals for its respective discipline, ranging from advanced engineering and technology systems to the social sciences, humanities, and strategic management.

## Document Structure
All official call documents are located within the `Convocatorias_WKS` directory. They have been meticulously organized and titled by their official area of focus and call number to ensure streamlined access, review, and strategic alignment for participating institutions, juries, and executive leadership.

This structure allows for seamless navigation and analysis of the comprehensive opportunities available within the 2026 World Knowledge Championship framework.
